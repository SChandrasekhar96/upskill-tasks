Welcome to Laundry Wallah, where we make laundry day a breeze! Our mission is to provide you with exceptional laundry and dry-cleaning services that save you time and ensure your clothes look their best.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Laundry Wallah began with a simple goal: to revolutionize the way people handle their laundry. Tired of the time-consuming and often tedious task of doing laundry, our founder, chandra sekhar, envisioned a service that would combine convenience, quality, and care. Today, we proudly serve India and beyond, helping busy individuals and families enjoy more free time and pristine wardrobes.
